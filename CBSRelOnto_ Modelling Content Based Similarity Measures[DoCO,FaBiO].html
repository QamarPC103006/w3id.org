Abstract

This ontology models content based similarity measures and those parameters which are used in such measures. The ontology helps in classification different relatedness measures and categorization of different content types involved in this relatedness measures.

Introduction

This ontology provides classifications for content based similarity measures. Beside that it aslo provides a hierarchy of content types of research papers for which these similarity measures are used. One major sub-category of content type is "Research Paper" and other is "Entities outside the research paper". Content base similarity measures are categorized as lexical, probabilisitic, visual, hybrid etc. Following figure represents the basic structure for this ontology.
